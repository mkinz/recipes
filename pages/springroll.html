Fresh spring rolls are a staple of our summer diet. They are light and fresh.

Sometimes it's too hot outside for anything else and nothing will do except for a fresh spring roll.
They are also really fun to make with a group!

Ingredients

  • Rice Paper
  • Water
  • Rice Noodles
  • Cucumber
  • Fried Tofu or Shrimp
  • Lettuce
  • Raddish
